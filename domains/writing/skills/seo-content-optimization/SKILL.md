# SEO Content Optimization

## Steps
1. Keyword research: identify primary and secondary keywords with search volume and competition data
2. Structure content with proper heading hierarchy (H1 → H2 → H3), include keywords naturally
3. Write meta title (≤60 chars) and meta description (≤160 chars) with primary keyword
4. Optimize readability: short paragraphs, bullet points, transition words
5. Include internal and external links to authoritative sources
6. Add alt text to images with descriptive keywords
7. Ensure mobile-friendly formatting and fast loading

## Examples
- Blog post: keyword "cloud migration best practices" → H1 uses phrase, H2s cover subtopics
- Product page: target "enterprise password manager" → meta title, description, body copy, and alt text
- Pillar page: comprehensive resource on a broad topic, linked to cluster content

## Anti-patterns
- Keyword stuffing (hurts readability and ranking)
- Neglecting search intent (writing content that doesn't match what the searcher wants)
- Thin content (under 300 words with no real value)
- Duplicate content across pages
